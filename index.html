DAY 1 GAMES
  • Sardines
    Vamp up your version of hide-and-seek!
  • SPUD
    Don't be the first one to spell out S-P-U-D!
  • Obstacle Course
    Use what you have around the house to create a set of challenges to work through as a team!
  • Hungry Snake!
    All you need is a jump rope and a cup of water to make it intersting
  • Jumbo Seasonal TIC-TAC-TOE
    Theme a life-size version of the historical game and personalize your pieces!
  • Winter Pictionary
    Use any large medium you have to write on and make your art specific to winter theme!
  • Penguin Shuffle
    You'll need a hacky sack style bag plus a couple eggs.
  • Snowball over the Mountain
    You can never use too many balloons for this kind of activitiy!
